Our Story

Formed in 2023 by Jalen Jones in Nashville, Tennessee, Osiris Development was created with the goal of creating fully customized websites for small businesses. Jalen's love for design and coding didn't come easily. After pursuing a career in piloting, Jalen switched his focus to software development as a different pathway to achieving his dream of being a pilot.

Today, Jalen and his team designs and develops websites for all types of businesses. As more clientele started doing business with us, we saw our demand skyrocket and started to market ourselves as a one-stop shop for everything website related. Because of this, we've been expanding nonstop to better serve our clients each and every day on every single project.
